## 🎯 About This Software

The hassio-addons project helps you control your smart home devices from your Windows computer. It connects your various gadgets so you can manage them in one place. You get a central dashboard to turn lights on, check locks, and set room temperatures. The software works in the background to keep your home running smoothly. 

You do not need programming knowledge to use this tool. It offers a simple interface for everyday tasks. 

## ⚙️ System Requirements

* Memory: 4 gigabytes of RAM or more.
* Storage: 500 megabytes of free disk space.
* Internet Connection: A stable connection for device updates.

## 🛠️ Setting Up Your Home

Once you download the file, you must run it. Find the downloaded file on your computer and double-click it. Windows may show a security prompt. If this happens, click "More info" and then "Run anyway" to allow the setup process to start.

Follow the instructions on your screen. The setup wizard asks where you want to keep the files. The default location is fine for most users. Click "Install" and wait for the status bar to finish. Click "Finish" to open the application.

## 🏠 Using the Dashboard

When the app opens, you will see a list of connected devices. If the list is empty, click the "Scan" button. The software searches your local network for compatible smart home hubs. Once it finds your gadgets, you can rename them. 

Pick a name that makes sense for each room or device. For example, change "Light-01" to "Living Room Lamp". Group your devices by room to help keep things organized. 

## 🔐 Managing Automation Rules

The automation tab lets you set schedules for your devices. You can tell your lights to turn on at sunset. You can also tell your thermostat to lower the heat when you leave the house. 

To create a new rule:

1. Click the "Add Rule" button.
2. Select your device from the dropdown menu.
3. Choose the action you want.
4. Set the time or event trigger.
5. Click "Save" to keep your settings.

## 🖥️ Maintaining the Software

The software checks for updates when you start it. If an update exists, click the "Update" button to get the latest features and security fixes. We recommend installing every update as soon as it appears. 

If you have issues, check the "Logs" tab. The logs show errors if a device fails to respond. Most connection errors relate to Wi-Fi signals or power problems. Make sure your devices stay plugged in and active at all times.

## ⚠️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

Sometimes devices show as "Offline". This often happens if the device loses its connection to the router. Restart your router if multiple rooms show as offline. If only one device shows as offline, check your power outlet first. 

If the application turns sluggish, close other open programs to free up system memory. You can also right-click the icon in your system tray and select "Refresh Connection" to force the app to talk to your smart home hub again.

This software runs on your local network. It does not monitor or send your data to external servers. This keeps your home habits private.

How safe is this software?
The software uses local encryption to talk to your hardware. No outside parties can access your device controls while you use it.

Yes, you can move the install folder after you finish the setup. You might need to update your desktop shortcut if you do this.
